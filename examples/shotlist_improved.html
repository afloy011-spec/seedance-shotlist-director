CUT 3 — Close-up profile, 85mm, slow push-in:
She tips the bottle back and drinks — throat working in long swallows, one bead of water cutting a clean line through the sweat on her chin. Eyes close. Shoulders finally drop. She lowers the bottle, exhales through rounded lips — and her thumb finds the LED ring again, resting on it.

ENDS ON: close on her hand around the bottle, thumb resting on the glowing aqua ring, her exhausted half-smile soft in the background. (Scene 2 match-cuts from this exact thumb-on-ring gesture.)

SFX: ragged breathing and slowing footfalls → crowd applause and barrier-board drumming at mid-distance → the seal-pop of the cap, long swallows, one deep exhale.
